Applicant Guidelines

Other Reminders
    For Teaching Personnel Applicants
  1. The applicant must be able to submit (1) hard copy of his/her transcript of records.
  2. The applicant must accomplish and yield satisfactory results from the following:
    1. Preliminary Interview with HR Officer
    2. Teaching demonstration before a screening committee
    3. Content test according to specialization
    4. Series of psychological tests given by the HR Officer.
  3. The Academic Coordinator will interview the applicant.
  4. The Principal/Director will conduct the final interview.

    For Non Teaching Personnel Applicants
  1. The applicant must be able to submit (1) hard copy of his/her transcript of records.
  2. The applicant will undergo preliminary interview and series of psychological tests with HR OFficer
  3. If the result is satisfatory, the applicant will have a recommendation from the HR Officer.
  4. The applicant will be interviewed by the Director.
    Admission Requirements
  1. Please bring the following requirements during the preliminary schedule interview.
    1. Original Transcript of Records.
    2. Photocopy of College Diploma.
    3. Certificate of employement from previous employer.
    4. BIR- Form 2316 from previous employer
    5. Photocopy of Seminar Certificates.
    6. Photocopy of PRC (for ATP), SSS, TIN, and Pag-IBIG IDs, MRD.
    7. Physical Examination Results.
    8. NBI Clearance.
    9. Birth Certificate
    10. Marriage Contract
    11. and Birth Certificates of Children(if applicable)

      References and Background Check
    1. Documents shall be subject to verificaiton for validity and authencity.
    2. Background investigastion shall also be conducted.
    3. Applicants found falsying information at the point of entry or after hiring shall be disqualified or terminated depending upon their status.
